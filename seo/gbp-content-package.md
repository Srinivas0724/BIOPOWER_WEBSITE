## Services

### Biogas Plants
Custom anaerobic digestion systems for residential, commercial, and institutional organic waste. Portable units from 1 kg/day to large-scale digesters producing biogas for kitchens, boilers, and power generation. Turnkey design, installation, and AMC across Bangalore and Karnataka.

### Bio-CNG Plants
Compressed Biogas (CBG) upgrading and bottling skids for food processors, municipalities, and entrepreneurs. SATAT-aligned solutions with methane purification, compression, and OMC offtake support. Ideal for 2+ TPD organic waste streams.

### Organic Waste Converters (OWC)
Fully automatic and semi-automatic composting machines for apartments, hotels, and campuses. Convert wet waste to odour-free compost in 24–48 hours. BBMP and SWM Rules compliance for bulk generators.

### Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)
Packaged MBBR, SBR, and tertiary treatment for apartments, industries, and townships. KSPCB-compliant effluent with water reuse for flushing and landscaping. Industrial ETP integration available.
