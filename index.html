Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our waste management and energy solutions.

What is a biogas plant and how does it work?

+

A biogas plant converts organic waste — food waste, agricultural residue, animal waste — into biogas through anaerobic digestion, producing clean renewable energy and organic manure as a by-product.

What scale of projects does BIOPOWER handle?

+

BIOPOWER operates across the full spectrum — from 1 kg/day household portable biogas plants to 100 metric ton/day institutional and industrial systems. We have the right solution for every scale.

What is Compressed Biogas (Bio-CNG) and how is it different from regular biogas?

+

Bio-CNG is raw biogas that has been purified and compressed to >99% methane content — equivalent to natural CNG. It can be used directly in CNG vehicles and industrial energy applications without engine modifications.

What is the typical payback period for an institutional biogas plant?

+

Institutional biogas plants typically achieve full return on investment within 16–20 months — making them one of the fastest-payback renewable energy investments available.

Are BIOPOWER systems suitable for homes and large industries alike?

+

Yes — BIOPOWER provides solutions for residential homes, apartments, hotels, hospitals, educational institutions, industries, and municipal projects. Our portfolio spans the full range from 1 kg/day to 100 MT/day.

Does BIOPOWER provide installation, maintenance, and after-sales support?

+

Yes — BIOPOWER offers complete turnkey support including feasibility studies, DPR preparation, design, manufacturing, installation, commissioning, and dedicated after-sales maintenance and monitoring.

What is an Organic Waste Converter (OWC)?

+

An Organic Waste Converter is a system designed to process biodegradable waste into compost through controlled microbial decomposition, reducing waste volume and supporting onsite waste management.
