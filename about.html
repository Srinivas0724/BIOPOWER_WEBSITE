ABOUT BIOPOWER

Bengaluru-based renewable energy leaders delivering end-to-end waste-to-energy solutions across India.

Our Mission

To enable a circular economy by converting organic waste into clean energy, compost, and reusable resources through innovative technologies.

Our Vision

To build a future of zero-waste, carbon-neutral communities powered by renewable energy and sustainable infrastructure.

Our Journey

Evolving for a Greener Future

BIOPOWER evolved from the sustainability initiatives of Synod Bioscience, active in waste management since 2011. Established as a dedicated waste-to-energy entity in 2022, we now execute turnkey projects across India, managing the entire lifecycle from waste processing to clean energy generation.

Our Capabilities

Integrated Solutions

Biogas Plants

Systems converting organic waste into clean fuel and nutrient-rich organic fertilizer.

Organic Waste Converters

Efficient composting systems transforming kitchen waste into nutrient-rich compost.

Compressed Biogas (CBG)

Technology upgrading biogas into high-purity fuel suitable for vehicles and industry.

Sewage Treatment (STP)

Advanced wastewater systems enabling safe water reuse and environmental compliance.
