Subsidy

Subsidy & Policy Information

Central and state programmes that may support your waste-to-energy project.

MNRE's National Biogas Programme and periodic state nodal schemes offer capital subsidy for community and institutional biogas. SATAT framework supports compressed biogas offtake through oil marketing companies. Eligibility varies by plant size, applicant category, and active notification period.

Read our detailed guide: Government Subsidies for Biogas Plants. BIOPOWER verifies current scheme status during your free site assessment and assists with technical documentation for subsidy applications.
